Meet Melissa Winterton, Thomas Winterton Wife

Thomas Winterton the star of The Secret Of Skinwalker Ranch is married to Melissa Winterton. Although they haven’t shared much about their marriage, Melissa and Thomas are likely married for more than one and a half decades.

Regardless, Thomas never misses celebrating his wife on social media. He has also gushed about her on several occasions. Like on Melissa’s birthday in 2022, Thomas wrote, “Happy Birthday to my best friend, my confidant, my partner in crime, my wife! So blessed to be at your side.”

The married couple is parents to three children including their son Kolby.

In the 2023 post for Roosevelt Utah Classifieds on Facebook Melissa shared that she wasn’t a big fan of dogs until her husband brought their dog named Porsche home. She wrote, “she stole my heart and her pups will steal yours too. Porsche weighs 40 pounds. Pups should mature between 30-45lbs.”

Kolby, the oldest of three turned 18 years old, on 27 September 2022. Melissa is very proud of her kid and wrote, “I couldn’t be more proud of the man he has become. A true leader, who has conducted himself throughout his teenage years with integrity and honor. He is a good person not because his parents have made him, but because he himself chose to be.”

Kolby attended Union High School where he played Boy’s Varsity Football. Kolby is a very skilled carpenter who back in 2021 won The cabinetry program at the Uintah Basin Technology College in Roosevelt, Utah.

At the time of the post in September 2022, Melissa and Thomas’s second born was 16 years old and 13 years old.

Talking about her husband, Thomas is part of History’s Skinwalker Ranch since 2020 appearing in a total of 30 episodes. Thomas has been serving in the capacity of Superintendent at the Skinwalker Ranch, supervising the upkeep of the facility and the events that take place there as well as helping with inquiries.

His program bio continues by stating that his special background and expertise have given him a thorough understanding of the UFO phenomenon at the ranch and the surrounding area. Thomas can be seen discussing his own incredible events that have occurred on the site since he joined in April 2016 in the program.

Thomas has worked as an entrepreneur in his hometown in addition to his position at the ranch. He has worked as a small business owner, general contractor, and project manager across a variety of industries. He has served as managing director of Intermountain Economic Consulting since January 2021.

Thomas is a founding member of a company that specializes in economic development. Its headquarters are in Salt Lake City, Utah, and it has a branch there as well. In addition, he serves as the CEO of Resonance Meditations, a company that offers life coaching, sound therapy, energy healing, and meditation services.

Thomas is the son of Lori and Ron Winterton, was born in 1981, and is 42 years old.
