Henry Fonda Net Worth

Henry Fonda was an iconic American actor born on May 16, 1905 in Grand Island, NE. He is best known for his starring roles in The Grapes of Wrath and Once Upon a Time in the West, among many other films. He was nominated for five Academy Awards and won one for his performance in On Golden Pond. He was also nominated for eight Golden Globe Awards and won two. Fonda's legacy as one of the greatest actors of all time lives on.
