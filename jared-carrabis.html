Jared Carrabis- Biography

Jared Carrabis was born in the United States on April 3, 1990. Podcast host and writer who is recognized for his work writing for Barstool Sports where he covers the Boston Red Sox and other baseball topics. He is also known for hosting the Barstool podcasts Starting 9 and Section 10.

He is credited with having helped David Price join the Boston Red Sox as the two were friends through Twitter. He started writing about the Boston Red Sox at 16 when he created the MySpace page SoxSpace where he wrote and covered the 2007 World Series.

He attended North Shore Community College before attending Endicott College. He published his first book One Fan’s Story in 2009. He got his first paying job in 2012 as a writer for the New England Baseball Journal.
