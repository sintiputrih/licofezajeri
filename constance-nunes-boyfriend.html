Constance Nunes Boyfriend, Bio, Career, Net Worth And More

Constance Nunes, the multifaceted star of the Netflix series “Car Masters: Rust to Riches,” is renowned for her skills as a model, car mechanic, TV personality, and entrepreneur. While her marriage to Jared Toller is part of her past, fans are eager to know about her current relationship status and more. Let’s delve into the details of Constance Nunes boyfriend, early life, career, and personal life, exploring the intricacies of her journey.

Constance Nunes Boyfriend

As of 2023, Constance Nunes is single and seemingly content. While entertaining her Instagram followers with captivating photos, there is no indication of a new relationship. Despite the passage of three years since her separation from Jared, Constance appears to be enjoying her independence.

Constance Nunes’s Early Life

Born on November 17, 1989, in Portugal and raised in California, Constance Nunes inherited her passion for automobiles from her father, Ernie Nunes. With a background rooted in her father’s drag racing and car-building ventures, she developed a deep love for fast cars and technical driving. After graduating from Thousand Oaks High School in California, Constance embarked on a path that seamlessly blended her love for cars with her career aspirations.

Constance Nunes’s Career as a Mechanic

Contrary to speculation, Constance Nunes is not just for show on “Car Masters: Rust to Riches.” With over 16 years of experience in the automotive industry, she gained hands-on knowledge assisting her father in his workshop. From working with top car manufacturers to collaborating with renowned companies like Ford, BMW, Audi, and Acura, Nunes’s expertise extends beyond the TV screen. She is currently associated with Gotham Garage in Temecula, California, showcasing her real-life skills as an engine specialist.

Constance Nunes’s Modeling Career

Constance’s journey into the fashion industry began in October 2008, initially selected as a model for racing events due to her frequent attendance at her father’s races. She represented brands such as Wranglers, Jockey, Jlux Label, Javanan Magazine, and Feral Cosmetics, making history as the first woman featured in one of Edelbrock’s national television commercials.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

As the owner of CARS by Constance in Wildomar, California, Constance Nunes fulfills her clients’ dreams of classic American car restoration, primarily focusing on 1960s-era Ford Mustangs. Her shop is a testament to her commitment to inspiring fellow automotive enthusiasts.

Constance Nunes’s Marriage and Separation

Constance married Jared Toller on February 19, 2019, but unfortunately, their marriage came to an end in 2020, with Jared filing for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences. Since then, Constance has been focusing on her personal growth and career.

Social Media Presence and Net Worth

Active on Instagram (@constance_nunes), Constance boasts a verified account with 1.2 million followers. Describing herself as a “glam girl wrenching in a ‘boys’ world” and the mother of #Babystang, she continues to engage with her audience. With a net worth of $2 million, Constance Nunes has not only made her mark on television but also in the automotive and modeling industries.

Conclusion

Constance Nunes’s journey unfolds as a captivating blend of passion, talent, and resilience. Beyond her on-screen persona, she continues to inspire through her entrepreneurial endeavors and commitment to the automotive world. While her relationship status remains private, fans can follow her on social media to catch glimpses of her thriving career and vibrant life.
