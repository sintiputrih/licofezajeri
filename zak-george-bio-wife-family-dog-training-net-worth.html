Zak George Bio

Zak George was born on December 3, 1978, in Atlanta, Georgia, U.S. He was born with the birth sign of Sagittarius. Zak is 40 years old as of 2019. He has been working as a dog trainer since 2006. He was invited in the show SuperFetch in 2009. Zak has also presented shows on channel Animal Planet. Moreover, he was associated with the show Who Let The Dogs Out which is basically for kids. This was aired on the CBBC channel since September 2011. Zak has been in different national shows to talk about dogs. Some of them include The Late Show with David Letterman, Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, The Early Show, Fox and Friends and many more. He wants to show the world about how dogs can do with a little bit of guidance. He owns his own YouTube channel where he has 1.8 million subscribers.
