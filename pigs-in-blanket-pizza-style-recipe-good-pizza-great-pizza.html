GPGP Pigs in Blanket Pizza Style Recipe

Pigs in Blanket Pizza-Style is a new Level 5 recipe and food item in GPGP. This is a very confusing food order, as pigs in a blanket usually means tiny hotdogs wrapped in dough. It is a very popular appetizer for parties and other such social gatherings. However, how would this even work in pizza form? Does the customer want a pizza that’s wrapped around meat in the same way as it works for traditional pigs in a blanket? Luckily, it’s much simpler than that.
