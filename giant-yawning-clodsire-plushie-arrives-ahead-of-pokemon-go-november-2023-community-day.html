Giant Yawning Clodsire Plushie arrives ahead of Pokmon GO November 2023 Community Day

Trainers and collectors, there’s a new must-have item for Pokémon enthusiasts, especially if you’re a fan of Clodsire and its pre-evolved form, Wooper. With the Pokémon GO Community Day event featuring Wooper and Paldean Wooper just around the corner this November 2023, Takara Tomy has timed the release of a new plushie perfectly.

Takara Tomy has just unveiled a new plushie that is sure to take up some significant shelf space. The plushie is a massive 35-inch Clodsire, depicting the beloved Pokémon in mid-yawn, radiating a level of coziness that’s hard to resist. What’s more, this plush giant comes with not one, but two companion Paldean Woopers, making it an irresistible trio that you cannot overlook.

This plush toy is priced at approximately $132 based on the current exchange rates, making it a valuable addition to your collection. The set is available for pre-order in Japan and is expected to be shipped in April 2024. If you’re interested in getting this cute and cuddly critter, make sure to mark your calendar and prepare for its arrival in the spring.

The dimensions of the Clodsire plushie, 42×63 cm, make it a sizeable addition to any Pokémon assortment. Whether you’re snuggled up in bed or gaming, this Clodsire plushie is perfect for Pokémon adventures.

Trainers, are you considering adding this charming Clodsire and its two Paldean Woopers to your collection of Pokémon memorabilia? This plush toy is not just any ordinary toy, it has a strong presence. Let’s admit it, who wouldn’t want to cuddle a sleepy Clodsire after a long day of catching Pokémon?
