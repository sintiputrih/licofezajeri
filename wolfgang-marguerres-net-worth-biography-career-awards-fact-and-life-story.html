Wolfgang Marguerres Net Worth, Biography, Career, Awards, Fact and Life Story

Net Worth:$2.7 Billion

Wolfgang Marguerre net worth: Wolfgang Marguerre is a German businessman and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $2.7 billion. Wolfgang Marguerre was born in Germany in 1941. He is the Chairman of the Octapharma Group. He founded the company with Robert Taub in 1983. The company is owned by Wolfgang and his three children. He earned his MBA and then worked as Managing Director at Pharmaplast. He then became the Director and Business Manager for Baxter-Travenol Europe. Marguerre became Senior Executive Vice-President of the Revlon Healthcare Group in 1979. In 2009 he awarded the Legion d’honneur which is the highest decoration bestowed by France. In 2011 he earned the Gold Decoration of Honour for Services to the Republic of Austria. He is responsible for turning Octapharma into one the world’s largest blood plasma product manufacturers. Wolfgang Marguerre donated $17.5 million to renovate the Theater Heidelberg. He lives in Heidelberg, Germany and is married with three children.
