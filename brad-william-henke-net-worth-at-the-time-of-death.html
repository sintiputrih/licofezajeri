Brad William Henke net worth at the time of death

A National Football League and Arena Football League player, Brad William Henke was an American actor who died on November 29, 2022.

His most notable performance was in the 2016 Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series for his portrayal of Corrections Officer Desi Piscatella on Orange Is the New Black.

Columbus, Nebraska is the place of Henke’s birth. He was a football player on the defensive line for the University of Arizona.

The New York Giants selected Henke in the NFL Draft in 1989, but he was released during training camp. In Super Bowl XXIV, he faced the San Francisco 49ers after being signed by the Denver Broncos. In 1994, he left professional football due to persistent problems that necessitated six ankle surgery.

Henke died in his sleep on November 29, 2022, at the age of 56, for no apparent reason. On May 15, 2021, he claimed in an Instagram post that his artery was 90% blocked.
