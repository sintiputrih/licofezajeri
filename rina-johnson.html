Rina Johnson Wiki, Age, Height, Weight, Biography, Family &amp; More

Rina Johnson (born in 2006) is an American Actress, Instagram Influencer, and Social Media Personality from Los Angeles, California. She is famous for playing one of the major parts in her debut film “The Crossbreed”.

It was released on the 6th of March 2018. Biray Dalkiran worked as a director as well as a co-writer of the movie. Furthermore, Safak Güçlü also worked as a writer of the project.

Rina played one of the recurring roles of Helen. Some of the other main casts of the horror film are Angela Durazo as Amy, Nathan Schellerup as John, Malinda Farrington as Maria, Danny Winn as Paul, etc.

Career, TV Shows & Net worth

The 13-years-old actress made her acting debut with the horror/thriller movie “The Crossbreed”. Her performance in this project was highly appreciated by many critics. Later, Johnson got an opportunity to play the lead character in the science fiction short film “Jump”.

In addition, the young artist acted as the main star of the movie called “The Girl”. which came out on 24 April 2018 (USA). The “Jump” project was directed and wrote by J. Van Auken.

Rina Johnson acted as V’s sister in BTS’s music video ON

Later, her next short film “Famous Little Girls” released on 11 August 2018. Rina portrayed the role of Young Marie Antoinette in the project. The other stars of the Famous Little Girls are Melanie Caplan, Sofia Kaempfe, Isabella Fowler, Hadley Fox, etc.

Johnson also spotted with BTS’s V for the group’s “ON” music video. BTS stands for Bangtan Boys which is a South Korean boy band of seven members. The members are Jimin, SUGA, V, Jungkook, Jin, RM, and J-Hope.

On Instagram, she told her audience that she portrayed the role of V’s sister who was missing in the music video. The accumulated Net worth of Rina Johnson is $300,000 US Dollars, as of 2020.
