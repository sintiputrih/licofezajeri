Kaia Gerber and Cindy Crawford Looked Like Twins in Matching Leather Jackets in NYC

  • On Sunday afternoon, Cindy Crawford and Kaia Gerber stepped out together for a peaceful stroll in New York City.
  • The mother/daughter models wore nearly-identical outfits for the outing, with both women sporting leather jackets (albeit of different styles), faded jeans, sunglasses, and white sneakers.

Kaia Gerber and her supermodel mom, Cindy Crawford, looked like actual twins during a walk around New York City Sunday afternoon.

The mother and daughter models wore very similar outfits, both of which included black leather jackets, light-wash jeans, sunglasses, and white sneakers.

While Gerber opted for her favorite Danielle Guizio leather trench coat, Crawford went with a cropped style for her leather look.

Both women sported faded jeans, but Gerber paired the look with a black top, while her mom went with a V-neck sweater. Crawford opted for white Stan Smith shoes and her daughter added a personal touch to the otherwise twinned-out ensemble with a low-key baseball cap (although it would take way, way more than sunglasses and a baseball cap to keep either of these women from being recognized on the street—especially together).

In a recent interview, the iconic supermodel opened up about her decision to let her daughter follow in her footsteps and enter the modeling industry as a teenager.

"I had no trepidation at all about saying yes to Kaia," Crawford said of letting Gerber start modeling at 16. "I felt comfortable and I was like, 'Go for it, have fun and I'm always here if you have a question.'"

Like mother, like daughter.
