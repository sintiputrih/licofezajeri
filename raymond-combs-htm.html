Raymond Combs Jr.'s About

Raymond Combs Jr. was born on April 3, 1956 (age 39) in Hamilton, Ohio, United States. He is a Celebrity Game Show Host. He is most famous for his role on CBS as second host of the popular game show Family Feud from 1988 until 1994. According to numerology, Raymond Combs Jr.'s Life Path Number is 1.
