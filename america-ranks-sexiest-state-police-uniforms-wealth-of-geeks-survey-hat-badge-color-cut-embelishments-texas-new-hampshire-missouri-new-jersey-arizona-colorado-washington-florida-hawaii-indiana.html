America ranks 'sexiest' state police uniforms

WASHINGTON (TND) — Many professions come with a uniform. Whether it's a polo shirt with a company logo, scrubs, or a heat-resistant jacket geared toward fighting fires, it's all part of the day's work.

Some uniforms, apparently, are more attractive than others.

A survey conducted by Wealth of Geeks, tracked the country's "sexiest state police uniforms," as voted for by 3,000 Americans.

The top ten consisted of Texas, New Hampshire, Missouri, New Jersey, Arizona, Colorado, Washington, Florida, Hawaii and Indiana.

Number one Texas's uniforms were described as "Lone Star Style," the surveyors saying "It's the cowboy hat that truly sets this uniform apart, worn with the swagger of a rodeo champion and the solemn duty of a ranger.
