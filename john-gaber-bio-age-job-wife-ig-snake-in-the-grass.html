John Gaber On Snake In The Grass

John Gaber is one of the 32 contestants in the show. “Getting to host Snake in the Grass has been a really cool experience,” Bobby said. “I lived in Costa Rica for more than a month while we were shooting it, so I can’t wait for everyone to finally get to see what we’ve been up to. John Gaber Age

In August 2022, John Gaber was 34 years old.

Does John Gaber Have A Wife?

No, John Gaber doesn’t have a wife, and likely doesn’t seem to be dating. Most of his recent social media posts are about working out.

John was previously engaged to his fiance Hannah. He has introduced his fiance on his vlogs multiple times. John proposed to Hannah in November 2019. The proposal was covered in Episode 560 of his vlog which was titled Proposal Day, that he uploaded on his YouTube.

Before the proposal, John asked Hannah’s father for permission to propose to his daughter.

In April 2020 vlog, John announced that he has split up with his fiance. He shared that they realized that they were two different personalities and they were forcing it to make things work.

In 2022, John is very private about his dating life. So, it is likely that he is focusing on his career rather than pursuing a love life.

John Gaber Job

John Gaber started his career in United States Air Force as a Pneudraulic Specialist. He served in the Air Force from September 2005 to October 20013. He stated that he was a maintenance technician on the hydraulic and pneumatic systems of the KC-135 strato-tanker. John held a 7 level at the rank of SSGT-E-5. AFSC-2A675.

From February 2008 to May 2018, he worked as a Maintenance Technician at Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company. In 2013, John also founded Iron Outcasts Gym LLC in Topeka, Kansas. He ran it till October 2018.

John is currently serving as the founder of Need Fitness LLC and C9LYFE LLC.

Furthermore, John is a social media star with more than 700 videos on his YouTube. His Instagram account earned him 167K followers in total which is helping him to establish his career as a motivational speaker.
