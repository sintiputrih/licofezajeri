What Is SugarHill Ddot Real Name?

Per Imdb, SugarHill Ddot real name is Darrian Jimenez. However, the rapper dislikes people using his real name.

One of the reasons why is due to the tragic passing of his friend, Notti Osama, back on July 9, 2022. Since then, the rapper and his most loyal followers have been hounding people to stop using his real name when appearing in the comments.

The rapper is from a Dominican family, which is evident by his last name but has not discussed his heritage in interviews. Instead, the rapper seems to take more pride in his city, his affiliations with his friends, and the music that he is making. 

As for his stage name, SugarHill Ddot, he represents his hometown, Sugarhill through the first name, while the last name comes from his real first name, Darrian.

Ddot is not the only rapper from his area to use the SugarHill name, as there is another rapper named SugarHill Keem, who he often collaborates with. 

Where Is Rapper SugarHill Ddot From?

SugarHill Ddot is from Harlem, New York. More specifically, he is from a neighborhood called Sugar Hill.

Sugar Hill used to be an affluent town back in the 1920s, during a period called the Harlem Renaissance. Many prominent and wealthy African Americans like activists Thurgood Marshall and Roy Wilkins, and musicians like Duke Ellington and Cab Calloway made their homes in the neighborhood.

However, the area had long since lost its luster when the rapper was born and brought up there. As mentioned above, the rapper had to hustle from a young age simply to make a living on the unforgiving streets of Harlem.

The violence in the streets affected SugarHill directly when his friend and collaborator, Nottie Osama, would get stabbed and passed away at the 137th Street/City College subway station on July 9, 2022.

As an altercation gone wrong, what happened to Nottie is just a small example of the rough life SugarHill has had to live.
