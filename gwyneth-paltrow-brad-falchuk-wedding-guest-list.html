Gwyneth Paltrow &amp; Brad Falchuk's Wedding Guest List Included Cameron Diaz &amp; Jerry Seinfeld

Gwyneth Paltrow officially tied knot with her TV producer fiancé, Brad Falchuk, this weekend. And just like the couple's engagement party back in April, Paltrow and Falchuk's wedding guest list was seriously star-studded.

Related Story

At the time, it was thought that Falchuk and Paltrow's glitzy engagement party might have been a secret wedding. Attended by everyone from Paltrow's mom, Blythe Danner (incidentally carrying a Monique Lhuillier bridal dress bag), to Jennifer Aniston, to director Steven Spielberg, per Daily Mail, the chic bash was organized by Falchuk's American Horror Story co-creator, Ryan Murphy.

But who was at Paltrow and Falchuk's wedding in the Hamptons this weekend? Here are all of the confirmed attendees:

Paltrow's bachelorette party organizer.

Back in April, Page Six reported that Cameron Diaz had organized a bachelorette party for Paltrow in Mexico. Diaz was seen arriving at Paltrow's wedding yesterday, along with her husband Benji Madden.

Dave M. Benett//Getty Images

A plethora of Hollywood celebrities.

A lot of famous faces stepped out for Falchuk and Paltrow's engagement party back in April, including Jennifer Aniston, Kate Hudson, Cameron Diaz, Julia Roberts, Liv Tyler, Reese Witherspoon, Demi Moore, Jennifer Aniston, and Chelsea Handler.

So far, celebrities seen arriving at the wedding included Iron Man star Robert Downey Jr., actor Rob Lowe, Cameron Diaz and her husband Benji Madden, and Steven Spielberg.

Comedian Jerry Seinfeld was photographed arriving at the wedding, having hosted Paltrow and Falchuk's rehearsal dinner the night before their nuptials. And Paltrow's mother, actress Blythe Danner, was photographed at the wedding wearing a stunning sparkly dress, per The Mirror.

American Horror Story, American Crime Story, and Glee cast and crew.

While it's likely co-creator, Ryan Murphy, attended Falchuk's wedding, along with members of the cast and crew from show's they've worked on together, there are no confirmed reports as yet.
