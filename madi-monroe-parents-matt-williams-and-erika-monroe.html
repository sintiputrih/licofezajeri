Who Are Madi Monroe Parents?

Meet Matt Williams, Madi Monroe Father

Madi Monroe thinks that her father Matt Williams is the “coolest dad.”

Matt is the son of Sally and Arthur Williams. They raised four boys together in Pine, California. Matt was the youngest addition to the family of six and was named Matthew Derrick. He idolized his older brothers as his objective “was to be them.”

They all received the high school’s best player title while playing eight-man football in Big Pine, a 900-person community on the eastern slope of the Sierras. Bart, his younger brother, was nine years older than Matt, thus Matt spent some time alone as an only kid while also having a role model to aspire to.

  • Matt Williams Age

Born on 28 November 1965, Matt Williams is 57 years old as of September 2023.

  • Matt Williams Net Worth

Matt Williams, according to Celebrity Net Worth, has amassed a fortune of $50 million. He is a retired MLB star.

Matt, a former third baseman and American professional baseball manager known by the monikers “The Big Marine” and “Matt the Bat,” is the third base coach for the San Diego Padres. Williams, a right-handed batter, competed for the San Francisco Giants, Cleveland Indians, and Arizona Diamondbacks in Major League Baseball. From 2014 to 2015, he was the Washington Nationals’ manager.

Matthew participated in a World Series for each of his teams: the Giants in 1989, the Indians in 1997, and the Diamondbacks in 2001, where he defeated the New York Yankees. He became the only player in Major League Baseball during this time to have at least one World Series home run for three separate teams.

Matt had a batting average of.268 overall, 378 home runs and 1,218 runs batted in (RBI) throughout his career. He participated in 1,866 regular-season games, amassed 1,878 hits, 338 doubles, and 35 triples, and scored 997 Major League runs.

Before the 2020 season, Matt joined the Kia Tigers of the KBO League and became their first manager who was born in the United States. After the team finished tenth with a 58-75 record in 2021, it was revealed on November 5, 2021, that Williams would not be joining the squad in 2022.

The San Diego Padres hired Matt on December 17, 2021, to be their third base coach for the upcoming 2022 campaign.
