In 2013, he began hosting the TV series 10 Things You Don't Know About.

About

Former frontman of the hardcore and punk band Black Flag, which disbanded in 1986. He also fronted The Rollins Band, known for songs like "Liar." In 2013, he began hosting the TV series 10 Things You Don't Know About. 

Before Fame

As a teenager, he performed in a variety of hardcore bands. He started attending American University but dropped out and worked minimum wage gigs while making music.

Trivia

Family Life

Associated With
