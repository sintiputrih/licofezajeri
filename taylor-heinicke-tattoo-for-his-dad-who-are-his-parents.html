Taylor Heinicke Tattoo For His Dad Who Are His Parents?

Taylor Heinicke got a tattoo for honouring his late dad. Find out how much impact his parents made on the athlete also about his salary and net worth

Taylor Heinicke is an American football player for Washington Football Team. He plays from the quarterback position.

Taylor is known for his incredible range, ball range and various techniques he utilizes. And he never misses out to honour his dad for making him the player he is now. After he died, unfortunately, he got a tattoo to commemorate his loss.

He recently replaced Ryan Fitzpatrick after Fitzpatrick went through a serious injury.

Taylor Heinicke Tattoo For His Dad

Two weeks after his dad died, Taylor Heinicke immediately got a tattoo for a physical representation of his father.

Heinicke got a tattoo of an angel pointing out to the cloud with the Bible verse  John 16:22 which reads as ‘Now is your time of grief, but I will see you again and you will rejoice, and no one will take away your joy.’. There’s also the word ‘DAD’ tattooed at the top as a heading.

Also, Taylor touches his tattoo, kisses it and points up to the sky every time he scores a ball. He does this to dedicate every scored point to his dad

Here, we can see young Taylor on the left with his father, Brett and his sister Lauren on the right.

Taylor Heinicke Family And Parents

Diane Dodsworth and Brett Heinicke are the parents of the athlete Taylor Heinicke. His family attends every game Taylor is in.

Although Taylor’s quality was recognized by his parents and coaches from a very early point in time, he did not get very much field run time. Fortunately, he was placed on the final match in senior high school (Collins Hill HS).

And, his parents, especially his father was able to see their son play in the field. Unfortunately, this was the last game for his father as he passed away 11 days later. Taylor says that he is incredibly lucky that his father could see him play before his time.
