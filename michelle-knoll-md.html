Dr. Michelle Knoll, MD Topeka, KS

Dr. Michelle Knoll is a pediatric endocrinologist affiliated with Children's Mercy Kansas City and Stormont Vail Hospital. She sees patients in Kansas City, MO and Topeka, KS. She specializes in patients with gender dysphoria and differences of sex development, as well as general pediatric endocrinology and diabetes.

She received her medical degree from University of Virginia School of Medicine.
