Audio Reveals Cardi B Yelling 'Both Y'all Wrong' After Allegedly Fight Between Quavo + Offset

Quavo and Offset reortedly got into it backstage at the 2023 Grammy Awards.

We previously reported that according to TMZ, before Quavo was set to take the stage to perform his song “Without You” as a tribute to Takeoff during the “In Memoriam” segment of the show- he and his former groupmate, Offset, got into a physical fight.

"The GRAMMYs had asked Offset to be a part of the tribute, but Quavo refused to let him take part" says TMZ.

According to Offset, the rumors are untrue. Offset reacts to the reports by tweeting, “what tf look like fighting my brother yal n*ggas is crazy…” While on the other hand, ET obtained some footage that allegedly shows Cardi B attempting to defuse the said situation. "Both of y'all wrong. This is not right," she's heard saying. "No b*****, shut the f**k up cuz you shouldn't have been talking."

When Cardi was asked what she was "settling," she jokingly replied, "settling? The only thing I settled was my outfit, honey."
