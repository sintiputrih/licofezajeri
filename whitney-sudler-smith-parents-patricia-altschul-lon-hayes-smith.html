  • Patricia Altschul Age

Patricia Altschul was born on April 16, 1941. That made her 81 years of age in 2022.

  • Patricia Altschul Net Worth

Patricia Altschul flaunted a net worth of $50 million in 2022.

An American socialite and reality television personality, Patricia isn’t just popular on TV. She was also well-known for being extremely active in the art community and as a real estate agent.

It was 1997 when Patricia first purchased a 30-room mansion in Long Island and sold the home for $12.5 million in 2009. In 2008, she then paid $4.8 million for the Isaac Jenkins Mikell House in Charleston — a 9,500 square foot home built in 1853 — and renovated it spending more than $5 million. For this, the star even received a Pro Merito Award/Carolopolis Award from the Preservation Society of Charleston.

Furthermore, her House was also listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

As for her education, Patricia attended Marymount School and Olney Friends School, and later graduated magna cum laude from George Washington University, earning a bachelor’s degree in 1964 and a master’s degree in 1965, both in archaeology and art history.

After completing her studies, Patricia then worked at George Washington University as an instructor. Soo, after, she then started a private art dealership called Arcadia, in Georgetown, and joined the world of real estate.
