Keshia Knight Pulliam is a famous American actress who came into limelight with her child appearance in The Cosby Show as Rudy Huxtable. She had appeared in 177 episodes. She then made a debut in movies in 1985 in The Last Dragon. Later in 2007, she was again appreciated for her role as Miranda Lucas-Payne in Tyler Perry‘s House of Payne. She then married Ed Hartwell in 2016 and got divorced in 2018. Keshia has always maintained her celebrity status and has kept her name out of controversies. Keshia, a single mother and the owner of Keshia’s Kitchen, feels that her family has always been at her side.
