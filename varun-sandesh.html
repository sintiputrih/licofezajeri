Wiki/Biography

Jeedigunta Varun Sandesh was born on 21 July 1989 (age: 30 years, as in 2019) in Rayagada, Odisha, India. His father was a cricket statistician. In his childhood, his family moved to Hyderabad and he spent his four years there. After that, his family immigrated to Bridgewater, New Jersey, U.S. from where he received his education. His zodiac sign is Cancer.

Career

Sandesh made his film debut in Telugu film industry with the superhit film, ‘Happy Days’ opposite Tamanna Bhatia.

Varun acted in Happy Days

He has also worked in many movies including Kotha Bangaru Lokam (2008), Evaraina Epudaina (2009), Kurradu (2009), Maro Charitra (2010), Kudirithe Kappu Coffee (2011), Chammak Challo (2013), Pandavulu Pandavulu Tummeda (2014), Mama Manchu Alludu Kanchu (2015), Mister 420 (2016), etc. For his third film, Evaraina Epudaina (2009), he also sang a song, “Vaanemo Thadisi.”

He along with his wife, Vithika Sheru, participated in Bigg Boss Telugu 2019.

Facts

  • His hobbies are playing Basketball, Cricket, Baseball, American football, Singing, etc.
