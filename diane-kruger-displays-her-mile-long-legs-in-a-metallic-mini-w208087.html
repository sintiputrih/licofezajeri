Diane Kruger Displays Her Mile-Long Legs In a Metallic Mini

Hometown hottie! Diane Kruger dazzled in her homeland during the premiere of her new film, Sky, in Berlin on Thursday, May 26. The 39-year-old actress shined just like the star she is in a figure-hugging metallic minidress. Watch today’s Red Carpet Daily, presented by AT&T, to see her look!

Kruger turned to Marc Jacobs for the custom creation. Her long-sleeved mirrored minidress showed off her totally toned legs and boasted a square neckline and strong shoulders. The metallic geometric-design also featured a sexy open back ,fastened with a bow tie at the neck. She stepped up the sizzling style even further in a pair of suede and calfskin Cesare Paciotti gladiator heels with a silver stiletto to match her dress.

On the day of the premiere, Kruger captioned an Instagram shot with a sweet sentiment in German: “War so schön dass es in Deutschland war,” which translates to, “Was so nice that it was in Germany.”
