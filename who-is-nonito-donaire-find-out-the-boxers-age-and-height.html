Who Is Nonito Donaire? Find Out The Boxers Age And Height

Nonito Donaire is the three times bantamweight champion. How old and tall is he? Stick to this article to learn about his age and height.

Nonito Donaire is a Filipino boxer where he is best known as The Filipino Flash because of his punching power and fast hand speed.

He is the oldest boxer in history who holds a three-time bantamweight champion. Moreover, he has won multiple world championships such as flyweight, featherweight, and the WBC bantamweight.

In 2021, he has secured the second position as an active bantamweight, where he has held nine world titles by the four major boxing sanctioning bodies.

Furthermore, he is one of the sixth prominent boxers who has won the world championship in three consecutive decades: 2000s, 2010s, and 2020s.
