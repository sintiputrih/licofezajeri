Khadijo Mohamed dies aged 74: Cause of death and obituary

Magnificent Political activist Khadijo Mohamed Diriye, who lived in Somalia from 2 April 1949 to 20 December 2023, held significant cabinet posts in the government.

Khadijo Mohamed Cause of death

According to a prime minister’s office source, Khadijo died after rupturing her pancreas after falling down stairs in a Djibouti hotel.

Opposition figures, family members, and representatives of civic society are demanding a thorough inquiry notwithstanding this explanation because they believe foul play may have occurred.

Ali Abdullahi Egal, also known as Ali Baashi, the deputy minister of security for Galmudug, voiced worries about the circumstances surrounding her death and demanded a thorough investigation.

The head of the Somali Journalists Syndicate, Abdalle Mumin, stressed the importance of conducting an independent inquiry and an autopsy.
