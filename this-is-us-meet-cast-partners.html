Meet the cast of This is Us and their real partners

Season six will soon be coming to a close

    This is Us has been a smash-hit with audiences since it first aired in 2016. The NBC show tells the story of the Pearson family over several generations and has made household names out of its incredible cast.

    MORE: This is Us creator opens up about potential spinoff series

    But what do you know about their lives away from the cameras? Find out everything you need to know about their previous and current relationships here...  

    Milo Ventimiglia

    Milo Ventimiglia leads the series as Pearson patriarch Jack, however, in real life, Milo is quite private about his own family life. While he has never been married, the 43-year-old actor has dated several of his former co-stars, including Gilmore Girls star Alexis Bledel and actress Hayden Panettiere. His last known relationship was with actress Diane Guerrero, but it is thought that he is now single.

    Mandy Moore

    Mandy Moore has been married to Dawes frontman Taylor Goldsmith since 2018. The couple - who share one-year-old son August together - met in 2015, not long after the actress filed for divorce from her first husband, Ryan Adams, who is also a musician. The former couple wed in 2009 but split on the grounds of "irreconcilable differences". Before that, Mandy also had a two-year relationship with star Zach Braff from 2004 to 2006.

    Justin Hartley

    Justin Hartley's love life has been in the spotlight in recent years due to his ex-wife Chrishell Stause's appearance on Selling Sunset. The couple had been married for two years when Justin filed for divorce during filming for the Netflix reality show's second season.

    He was first married to actress Lindsay Korman, with whom he shares a 16-year-old daughter, and he is now wed to his former co-star Sofia Pernas.

    Chrissy Metz

    Chrissy Metz was previously married to British journalist Martyn Eaden, who she met via a dating app in 2006. The couple exchanged messages for two years before tying the knot in a ceremony in Santa Barbara, California. Sadly, the couple split in 2013 and were officially divorced in 2015.     Sterling K. Brown

    Sterling K. Brown has been married to actress Ryan Michelle Bathe since 2007. The couple met at college in the early twenties and had their many ups and downs, including several breakups, in the early stages of their relationship. Ryan has even joined Sterling on playing Yvette, the mother of one of young Randall's friends.

    Chris Sullivan

    Toby, Kate's husband, is played by actor Chris Sullivan who has been married to producer Rachel Sullivan since 2010.

    Jon Huertas

    In the series, Rebecca's second husband, Miguel, is played by Jon Huertas, who tied the knot with his long-term girlfriend, Nicole Bordges, back in 2014.

    Ron Cephas Jones

    Ron Cephas Jones plays William "Shakespeare" Hill, Randall's biological father in the show's earlier seasons. In real life, he is married to British jazz singer Kim Lesley.
