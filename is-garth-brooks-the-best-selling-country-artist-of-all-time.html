Is Garth Brooks the best-selling country artist of all time?

Garth Brooks is an American country musician who has gained widespread recognition.

Brooks’ success has been attributed to a number of factors, including his strong songwriting, his charismatic stage presence, and his ability to connect with a wide range of fans.

He has won numerous awards, including 17 Grammy Awards, and has been inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame. He is considered one of the most influential and popular country artists of all time.

Is Garth Brooks the best-selling country artist of all time?

Garth Brooks is indeed regarded as the best-selling country artist of all time.

According to the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), he has sold over 157 million albums in the United States, more than any other solo artist in any genre.
