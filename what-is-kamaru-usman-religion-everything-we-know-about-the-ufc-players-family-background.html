What Is Kamaru Usman Religion? Everything We Know About The UFC Players Family Background

Kamaru Usman is a professional mixed martial artist currently competing in the welterweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). There he is, the reigning UFC Welterweight Champion.

Usman is also a former freestyle wrestler and graduated folkstyle wrestler. Besides, he has also served as the wrestling coach for Team Miller in The Ultimate Fighter season fourteen in 2011.

On December 19, 2015, Usman made his official debut as a UFC athlete, where he faced future contender Leon Edwards and Usman won the match by unanimous decision. He started his wrestling career in his sophomore year in high school at Bowie High School in Arlington, Texas.

For one year, he also wrestled in Iowa at William Penn University. In 2007, Usman was an NAIA national tournament qualifier but was unable to attend the tournament due to a snowstorm.

Kamaru Usman Family Background And Heritage

Kamaru Usman was born in Auchi, Nigeria, to his father, Muhammed Nasiru, and mother, whose details are yet to get covered. His dad was a major in the Nigerian Army, while his mom worked as a teacher.

Moreover, Usman holds an American nationality and belongs to Nigerian-American heritage. Further, Usman moved to the US at the age of eight when his father became a pharmacist in the United States.

Similarly, he was raised alongside his two brothers, Kashetu and Mohammed. Additionally, Kashetu is a Doctor of Pharmacy, and Mohammed is a mixed martial artist.
