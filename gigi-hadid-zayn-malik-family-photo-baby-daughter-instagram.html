Gigi Hadid &amp; Zayn Malik Post First Family Photo with New Baby

While celebrating Halloween as a family of three for the first time, Gigi Hadid and Zayn Malik looked seriously loved-up.

In celebration of Halloween 2020, Hadid shared several snaps of her incredible, skintight costume on social media. She also posed with her beau, Malik, who just so happened to be holding their new baby. Both Hadid and Malik gazed adoringly at their daughter in the snap, proving that family life is treating them well right now. The model aptly captioned the picture with a sticker which read, "My First Halloween."

The private couple obscured their baby's face on Instagram, but it's clear that mom and dad are totally smitten with their new arrival.

Instagram

In September, Malik shared the news of his daughter's birth in an adorable Twitter post. "Our baby girl is here, healthy & beautiful," he wrote. "To try put into words how I am feeling right now would be an impossible task. The love I feel for this tiny human is beyond my understanding. Grateful to know her, proud to call her mine, & thankful for the life we will have together x."

Meanwhile, a source revealed to E! News that Hadid is loving motherhood so far. "Gigi is very tired but is already an amazing mom," the source explained. "The first couple of weeks have been a hard transition but she hasn't complained and is very elated to be a mom... Gigi wants the most privacy for their baby and wants to be able to raise her privately."
