Who is Kirk Cousins? Early Life and Career

Before we get to know Kirk Cousins’ net worth and salary, let’s get to know more about his early life and career. Kirk Daniel Cousins, an American football quarterback for the Minnesota Vikings of the National Football League (NFL), was born on August 19, 1988.

After Griffin sustained an injury in his first three seasons, Cousins briefly entered games before taking Griffin’s place as the team’s starter in 2015. He held that position through the end of the 2017 season.

Kirk Cousins established multiple team records while playing for the Redskins and was selected for the 2017 Pro Bowl.
