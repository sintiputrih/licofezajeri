Herb Kelleher Net Worth

Herb Kelleher was an American businessman and co-founder of Southwest Airlines. Born on March 12, 1931 in New Jersey, Kelleher was a pioneer in the airline industry, leading Southwest Airlines to become one of the most successful airlines in the United States. He served as Chairman Emeritus of Southwest Airlines until his death in 2019. Kelleher's legacy lives on in the success of Southwest Airlines and his commitment to customer service.
