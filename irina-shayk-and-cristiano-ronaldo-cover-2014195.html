Cristiano Ronaldo Naked on the Cover of Vogue Spain with Girlfriend

Vogue may be known for its high-fashion photo shoots and gorgeous clothes, but hunky soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo appears on the cover of the latest issue of Vogue Spain wearing nothing at all. 

Ronaldo, 29, models for the front of the June 2014 issue of Vogue España with his girlfriend, Irina Shayk. While the soccer star is completely nude, supermodel Shayk, 28, wears a stunning white strapless gown with a slit up the thigh. 

Ronaldo is posed with his hands on his hips and his stance wide, his supermodel love standing in front of him, offering just a peek at his inner thigh and keeping the cover from becoming X-rated. With his eyebrows well-manicured and his face close-shaven, Ronaldo wears just a diamond stud earring in his ear. 

Photographed by fashion photographer Mario Testino, the gorgeous couple's cover reads "Amor & Fútbol," or "Love and Soccer." 

Ronaldo, who is a forward for Real Madrid, has been dating the Russian model since 2010. The Vogue España cover is the duo's first as a couple and was styled by fashion legend Carlyne Cerf de Dudzeele.
