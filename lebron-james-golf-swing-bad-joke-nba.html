LeBron James shows off 'vile' golf swing that NBA fans joke is so bad it 'may affect his legacy'

BASKETBALL legend LeBron James showed off his golf skills - and fans told him to "stick to NBA."

The 37-year-old Los Angeles Laker heads into the new season chasing Kareem Abdul-Jabbar's all-time scoring record.

James will be hoping the Lakers can put together a strong campaign after missing out on the playoffs in 2021.

LeBron is back in training ahead of LA's first game later this month and was spotted hitting Top Golf in Las Vegas during some downtime.

The four-time NBA MVP was filmed trying out his swing in the Black Clover Suite.

But his efforts were roundly mocked by golfers after producing what one commenter termed a "vile" effort.
