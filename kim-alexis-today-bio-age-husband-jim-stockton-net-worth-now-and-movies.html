Kim Alexis Biography

Kim Alexis is a celebrated American Supermodel and Actress who was born and brought-up in Lockport, New York. She gained wide recognition and widely known in the 1970 and 1980. She has appeared on numerous and popular cover of magazines such as ‘Sports Illustrated’, ‘Vogue’, ‘Harper’s Bazaar’ and ‘Cosmopolitan’.

Kim also known for her roles on notable films and shows including ‘Healthy Kids’ on the Family Channel, VH1’s reality competition ‘But Can They Sing?’ and as the host of ‘Daily Edition’. She advertised ‘Alpine Lace Brands, Inc.’ as the spokeswoman promoting the company’s Alpine Lace low-fat cheese products in 1995.
