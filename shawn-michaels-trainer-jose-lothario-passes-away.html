Shawn Michaels Trainer Jose Lothario Passes Away

Jose Lothario, who trained and managed Shawn Michaels in the WWE during 1996-1997, has passed away. R. Bruce Tharpe, who is the former president and owner of National Wrestling Alliance made the announcement moments ago on Twitter.

No details have emerged of his cause of death, but Lothario was 83 years old. We will keep you updated if we receive any additional information.
