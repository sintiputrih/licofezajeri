Johnny Weir Eliminated From 'Dancing With The Stars'

Johhny Weir is one of the reality series contestants, Dancing With The Stars, who recently got eliminated. While he was still in the reality series, he massively attained praise from the judging panel, which was not enough to take him to the finals. Eventually, Johnny Weir and his partner Britt Stewart packed their bags to take off to their home.

Along with them, Skai Jackson and Alan Bersten also went home in a double elimination.

Johnny Weir and his partner, Britt Stewart (©: Instagram/JohnnyWeir)

It is clear that Weir is not happy with the results from the past week and also called Dancing With The Stars a contest of popularity. Speaking with US Weekly, he mentioned, 'It hurts a lot because it definitely feels like a popularity contest more so than a dance contest, but that's part of it.' He added, 'I knew what I was signing up for. I can say wholeheartedly that I would always prefer to be myself than be popular.'

Despite that, he said that he had no regrets in competing in the dancing reality series. Also, Weir said that he and his partner, Britt Stewart did what was right for them throughout their time in Dancing With The Stars.

Who Is Johnny Weir? His Net Worth

Johnny Weir is a professional American figure skater and a television commentator who has accumulated a net worth of $4 million throughout his professional career.

Weir is widely acknowledged as a two-time Olympian, who won a bronze medal in the 2008 World Skating. Similarly, he is a two-time Grand Prix Final bronze medalist and a three-time United States National Champion from 2004 to 2006.

Johnny Weir as a figure skater (©: NBC News)

Moreover, he is the 2001 World Junior Champion. Currently, he serves as a commentator for skating competitions, including the Olympics alongside Tara Lipinski.

More About Johnny Weir; Is He Married?

Johnny Weir was born on 2 July 1984 in Coatesville, Philadelphia, in the United States. He attended the University of Delaware but dropped out later to pursue his skating career. He grew up with his brother named Brian Weir, including his parents, John Weir and Patti Weir.

Concerning his physical features, he stands at a decent height of 5 feet and 9 inches tall and weighs 68 kg or 150 lbs.

Talking about his romantic ventures, no details have erupted. He leads a subtle profile regarding the love matter, and there are no signs of his past relationship. With that, it clearly means that Johnny Weir is not married.
