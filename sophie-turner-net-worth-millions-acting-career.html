How Sophie Turner Amassed Her $10 Million Net Worth Since 'Game Of Thrones'

Sophie Turner is a beloved actress and model, most known for her role as Sansa Stark in one of the most popular shows in history. By the time the Game of Thrones season finale aired, Turner had bagged several nominations, including a People's Choice Award and an Emmy Award.

Apart from the HBO series, the British actress has worked on other projects that have grown her career and net worth to what it is now. Turner fell in love with one of the Jonas Brothers and married in 2019; she and Joe Jonas have since welcomed two kids together. So, here’s how Sophie Turner grew her net worth to $10 million after her first TV role.

12 Sophie Turner’s Early Acting Career

Sophie Turner was born in the United Kingdom on February 21, 1996. She got into acting at the early age of three,f when she joined the Playbox Theatre Company. Turner’s breakout role came when she landed the part of Sansa Stark in HBO’s Game of Thrones.

11 Sophie Turner Earned $2 Million Per Episode For Game Of Thrones

Sophie Turner is mostly known for her role as Jon Snow's half-sister in the HBO original series based on George R.R. Martin novels. The series quickly became one of the world’s biggest shows on the streaming site, and with that came a huge paycheck for Turner.

The 26-year-old actor appeared in 59 episodes, during which she earned an Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series. According to Indiewire, Turner earned close to £158,000 per episode for Game of Thrones. With this income, it’s no surprise that the mother of two made a huge part of her wealth working with HBO.

10 Sophie Turner Joined The X-Men Franchise For $2 Million

Besides Game of Thrones, Sophie also joined another huge production, X-Men. In 2016, she debuted as Jean Grey in X-Men: Apocalypse and was reportedly paid $2 million.

The first film starred James McAvoy and Jennifer Lawrence and only made $533 million at the box office. Its sequel, 2019’s X-Men Dark Phoenix, didn’t do as well and only grossed $252 million at the box office.

9 Sophie Turner Is A Louis Vuitton Ambassador

Like many high-profile celebrities, Sophie Turner was picked as Louis Vuitton's brand ambassador. Turner’s salary for her longtime deal with the fashion brand hasn’t been disclosed yet, but actress Emma Stone was paid $12.8 million so it's probably in that range. The Game of Thrones actress also featured in the brand’s recent Tambour Street Diver watch campaign, so her deal with the brand is still earning her good money.

8 Sophie Turner Is The Face Of Wella Professionals.

In 2017, Sophie Turner signed a deal with the beauty brand Wella Hair to join the company as their brand ambassador. Sophie is undoubtedly earning a nice salary from the exclusive deal, seeing as she always posts Wella Hair ads on her Instagram.

7 Sophie Turner’s Films And TV Appearances

In 2018, Turner returned to acting with the sci-fi film Time Freak and Josie, released the same year. The next year, Sophie appeared in one film, Heavy, before doing the series Survive. She also appeared in HBO Max’s 2021 animation The Prince as Princess Charlotte, which received mixed reviews from fans and critics of the Royal family.

Sophie’s most recent role was in another HBO project - a true-crime series, The Staircase where she plays the daughter of novelist Michael Peterson who was convicted of murdering his wife in 2001.

6 Sophie Turner’s Modeling Career

Sophie Turner earns money as a model due to her height (1.75m) and striking good looks. In 2014, Turner made her modeling debut in Karen Millen’s Fall autumn/winter campaign and has recently graced the covers of magazines like Elle UK, Marie Claire UK, Vogue, Rolling Stone and Harper’s Bazaar.

5 Sophie Turner Earns Money From Her Instagram

The X-Men star has an impressive social media following, with 14.4 million followers on Instagram. Like most actresses with a huge following, she gets paid money to promote different brands to her audience. Sophie has so far done ads for brands like, Fortnite, Louis Vuitton and Audi, who signed lucrative contracts to work with her.

4 All The Homes Sophie Turner Has Owned

The Dark Phoenix actress and Joe Jonas owned a Miami mansion that they sold for $15 million, $4 million more than they paid for it two years earlier. Before this, the couple had spent 14 million on an Encino mansion that they listed for $15.2 million.

Sophie is set to retain ownership of any homes she purchased before her marriage now that she is splitting from Joe Jonas. The actress likely owns property in London, where she expressed desire to return for the sake of her mental health.

3 Sophie Turner Has An Expensive Car Collection

Sophie Turner's net worth includes several vehicles that she has acquired throughout her acting career. Turner owns nine vehicles that are worth more than $400,000. Her vehicles include a $145,990 BMW i8, a $123,500 Mercedes S-Class, a $100,900 Porsche Cayenne and a $95,890 Cadillac Escalade.

2 Will Sophie Turner’s Net Worth Increase After Divorce?

At the height of Sophie Turner’s career, Joe Jonas sent her a DM, and they began dating. Sophie and Joe dated for a few years before they got hitched in 2019 in an impromptu Las Vegas ceremony. The former couple have two daughters together, aged 3 and 1. After weeks of speculation, on September 5, 2023, Sophie and Joe released a joint statement explaining to their fans that they were splitting up.

The DNCE singer filed for divorce with court documents citing that their marriage was “irretrievably broken.” After four years of marriage, Sophie will leave the marriage with all her money earned before the relationship. And so will Joe Jonas because of the ‘iron clad’ prenup they had in place.

1 Sophie Turner’s Future Work

Since her appearance in Game of Thrones and X-Men, Sophie Turner has been working on more interesting projects. Despite the drama in her personal life, she is set to star in a television series, Joan, that she has been filming in the UK since May 2023.
