Get To Know Physical 100 Tarzan Age, Job, Height, Real Name

The Australian Tarzan knows what to do when an opportunity such as to participate in Physical 100 arises — join in and slay the challenges!

So, while he does that, let’s take a look at his personal life over this Physical 100 Tarzan Bio.

Meet Tarzan, One Of The Contestants On Netflix’s Physical 100

Be it the show’s resemblance to Squid Game or the mixture of stars, Netflix’s Physical 100 grabbed the No. 7 spot on Netflix’s top 10 TV shows list, within a week of its premiere (on January 24, 2023). Well, creator Jang Ho-gi was surely happy as he was the one to put together 100 contestants — including Tarzan — from various ethnicities, ages, and genders as they competed against each other to prove their physical strength and ultimately for a chance to win the title of “most perfect physique” plus 300 million won (approx USD 243 thousand).

“Fight to the death. Survive to the end.” These were the words that appeared in the show’s trailer. The contestants competed to survive purely with their physical strength.

In the first ep, Tarzan and other contestants were placed in a massive space where plaster casts of their torsos sat on marble pedestals, arranged in concentric circles around a fountain. They then walked around, admiring each other’s plaster/actual bodies, objectifying and judging each other.

So, for the first challenge, the contestants then were asked to hang on to a suspended apparatus lifted in the air. Through this, the participants quickly realized that the show was not only a test of brute strength but also endurance and mental strength.

Later, based on how long they lasted, the players were then placed in order from 100 to 1, and in order, they got to choose who they’ll face in the first quest — a  fight over a ball.

Overall, 50 matches took place in the same two arenas, just like The Challenge. Yet Physical 100 stood apart from other gaming shows staging these in much more interesting ways and with a massive cast.

Physical 100 Tarzan Age

Physical 100 Tarzan was reportedly born before 1995. That made him at least 27 years of age when he appeared on Physical 100 in 2023.

He certainly was one of the younger contestants that season.

What Is Physical 100 Tarzan Real Name?

Physical 100 Tarzan’s real name is “Hyunjun Seok.”

He was dubbed the Australian Tarzan for his fitness and outstanding physical ability.

Physical 100 Tarzan Job

Physical 100 Tarzan is a travel YouTuber. His Youtube page is named Modern Tarzan and there he shares travel vlogs and some of his fitness routine. As of 2023, he had already garnered a following of over 138K subscribers.

Besides being a Youtube star, Tarzan also is the business owner of the clothing brand Fitness Paradise. He started the company in 2022 with a vision to create multifunctional athleisure clothing.  “We pursue fashion that not only compliments your look but also improves your gym & workout experience. It is our goal to become a brand that can serve millions of customers around the globe with our quality gym wear and lifestyle products,” his site explained.

Most of his clothing articles included tops and shorts. It ranged anywhere from KRW 42,000 to KRW 119,600.

The Youtuber also modeled the items on the brand’s Instagram page @fitnessparadise_official.

Now, Tarzan can also be labeled as a reality star after his appearance in Physical 100. He even has content on his Youtube from the day of filming the Netflix show with Agent H. Later, after gracing the show’s premiere, the star also uploaded another video on Youtube from the eventful day.

And not to mention, he often promoted the show on his IG as well. “Everyone, have a good rest of the Lunar New Year holiday, and see you at 5:00!! <Physical: 100 Tarzan Poster> Tarzan is coming out at 5 pm in Korea time,” he posted on Jan 24, 2023.

As for his income as a Youtuber, Tarzan said that his first-ever earnings were around 100K KRW. Now, he earns a lot more (mostly depending on the views).

Did you know: Tarzan has been featured in a Samsung Ad and MensHealthKorea in 2020.

Physical 100 Tarzan Height

Physical 100 Tarzan stands tall at a height of 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm) and weighs 79 kg (174 lbs).

His distinct features include — long hair and a ripped body.

Trivia: Tarzan lost his loving grandmother on March 19, 2022.
